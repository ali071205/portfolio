River Front Resort

Part 1 — Concept & Vision

Concept

Project Vision

Integration of modern resort design with natural riverfront surroundings. Balance privacy, community, shade and sun.

Highlights

Eco approach, landscape-first layout, river-facing decks and floating elements anchored with elastic docks.

Hero

Imagine a slow morning by the river: boardwalks, shaded courtyards and low massing that follow the terrain. The design prioritizes natural ventilation, short pedestrian paths and framed views.

Philosophy

Nature integration. Architecture follows terrain, shade and breeze. Sustainability. Passive cooling, STP, rainwater harvesting, local materials. Balanced living. Options for privacy and communal life.
