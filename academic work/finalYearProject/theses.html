Concept Image

Visualizing the blend of natural elements and architectural strategy

Project Quick Overview

Site

12.38 acres on the Kozhikode coast. Strategy: keep heavy mass inland, float light elements on water, preserve drainage and trees.

Strategy

Passive cooling, local materials (laterite, bamboo), ferrocement shells, and a closed-loop water cycle with STP and rainwater harvesting.
