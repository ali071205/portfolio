Architectural Showcase

Main Block. Entry court, double-height lobby, restaurant, banquet hall. Service spines behind public rooms.

Hotel Block. Typical floors stack compact rooms along a 3m corridor with daylight pockets.

Wellness Center. Hydrotherapy at the core, massage rooms facing courts, stack ventilation in sections.

Engineering

Floating Systems. Pontoon logic: foam-filled modules or reinforced concrete pontoons. Services run in protected chases above water.

Anchoring. Elastic dock systems that allow movement during surge and reduce peak forces.

Materials & Construction. Ferrocement shells for durability in salt air, bamboo frames for light loads, simple details for local repair.
