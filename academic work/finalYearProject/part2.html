The Site — 12.38 Acres

Geography. Kozhikode coast. Strategy: heavy mass inland, light floating elements by water, keep drainage lines.

Access. Road, rail (Kozhikode), air (Calicut). Last mile: car, auto or boat transfer in high water.

Climate. Warm humid summers, monsoon rains, pleasant winters—design fights humidity with shade and ventilation.

SWOT Summary

Strengths

Prime coastal setting, low-rise plan, native planting.

Weaknesses

CRZ limits and high water table; mitigated by light structures and careful siting.

Opportunities

Eco tourism, local partnerships and research tie-ups.

Threats

Storm surge and sea-level rise; response includes raised boardwalks and evacuation routes.

Sustainability Report

Energy. Fan-first, passive cooling, solar hot water.

Water. Rain storage, STP for reuse in irrigation and flushing.

Materials. Ferrocement, bamboo, laterite and low-VOC finishes.

Waste. Composting, oil collection, optimized linen cycles.
