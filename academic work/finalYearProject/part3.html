Accommodations

Floating Villas

Private decks, living room opening to river, plunge pool screened by bamboo. Access by short boat ride.

Land Cottages

Shaded verandas, cross ventilation, small courtyards and light insulated roofs.

Hotel Rooms & Suites

Efficient layouts stacked along service cores; deluxe rooms add lounge and views.

Dining

Floating Restaurant

Tables over water, coastal menu with local produce.

Main Restaurant

Open kitchens, local grains, bakery and low-waste cooking.

Bars & Tea

Small riverfront bars and a tea pavilion for all-day snacks and drinks.
