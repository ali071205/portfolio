FAQs

How do I reach the resort? Road from Kozhikode, rail, or air via Calicut International plus short transfer.

Best season? Winter is best, monsoon is lush, summer is hot—choose shaded activities.

Child friendly? Yes: kids’ zone and shallow pools.

Travel Guide

Day trips: Beypore, Kappad, and nearby hills. Respect local customs and support small vendors.
